About the Project

World Runner is a game-based art project about the human life cycle. It shows how people spend most of their adult lives running after temporary things like success, money, and stability. The character keeps running until the world fades into a void, showing that life eventually ends in something unknown. The project is meant to make viewers think about what people chase in life and what truly remains at the end.
